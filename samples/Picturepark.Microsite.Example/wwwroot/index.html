Picturepark Case Studies

  • Faber-Castell has been the world’s best known manufacturer of precision writing instruments for centuries. The company relies on best-of-breed enterprise resource planning, digital asset management and web content management systems to take product data from the drawing board to the market.

    Faber-Castell Case Study


  • Arena has grown into a global icon of swimwear preferred by many world-class and Olympic athletes. Arena uses Picturepark DAM to power the company’s 24/7 global content distribution that helps drives the brand.

    Arena Case Study


  • Infront works with more than 160 rights holders and relies on Picturepark digital asset management software to manage an impressive library of business assets.

    Infront Sports Case Study

    Belden Case Study
